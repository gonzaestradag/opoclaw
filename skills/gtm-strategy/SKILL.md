# gtm-strategy

Build a concrete, actionable GTM plan. No fluff frameworks — just the decisions and moves. Designed for Aria (strategy).

## Triggers

Use when launching something new: a product, a service, a feature, or entering a new vertical.

## Required inputs (ask if not given)

1. What is the product/service?
2. Who is the ideal customer? (be specific — not "businesses" but "solo founders running service businesses")
3. What problem does it solve?
4. What's the business model? (one-time, subscription, per-use, etc.)
5. Any existing audience, warm contacts, or channels?

## Output structure

### 1. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Exact demographic + psychographic
- Where they hang out online
- What they search for / talk about

### 2. Positioning
- One-line positioning statement
- What category are we in?
- Why us vs alternatives?

### 3. Channel stack (ranked by speed/cost)
- Top 3 channels with specific tactics for each
- Organic vs paid breakdown

### 4. First 30 days — concrete moves
- Week 1: [specific actions]
- Week 2: [specific actions]
- Week 3-4: [specific actions]

### 5. Success metrics
- What does "working" look like at day 30, day 90?

## Output tone

CEO-level: outcomes and decisions, not process. Flag the highest-leverage action to take TODAY.
