# lead-magnet

Design high-converting lead magnets for Gonzalo's ventures. Designed for Aria (strategy) and Sofia (content) in OpoClaw.

## What makes a great lead magnet

The Hormozi test: Would someone pay for this if you charged $20? If yes, give it free.

- **Specificity beats breadth**: "5 scripts to close consulting calls" > "Sales guide"
- **Immediate value**: delivers a win in under 10 minutes
- **Pre-sells the main offer**: the free thing shows what the paid thing is like
- **Low cost to produce**: templates, checklists, scripts > courses

## Format options (ranked by conversion)

1. **Template/toolkit** — ready-to-use, fills in blanks (highest conversion)
2. **Cheat sheet/checklist** — 1-page reference they keep
3. **Mini case study** — "How we did X in Y days"
4. **Script/swipe file** — copy-paste ready
5. **Video walkthrough** — shows the process, not just describes it
6. **Quiz/assessment** — interactive, gives them a score

## Design process

```
1. Who is the exact target? (be hyper-specific)
2. What is their #1 frustration RIGHT NOW?
3. What is the quickest win we can give them on that?
4. What format delivers that win fastest?
5. What's the title? (Formula: [Number] + [Specific Result] + [Time Frame])
   Example: "7 Cold Email Scripts That Booked 43 Meetings in 30 Days"
6. What is the CTA after they get it? (next step toward paid offer)
```

## Output

Provide:

- Outline of content
- Distribution channel recommendation (where to put it)
- Follow-up sequence hook (first email they get after downloading)
