VisionClaw — What You Need to Do in Xcode
==========================================

Everything has been set up programmatically. You just need to do these 4 steps:


STEP 1 — Open the project
--------------------------
In Xcode: File > Open
Navigate to this folder (VisionClaw on your Desktop)
Open the file: CameraAccess.xcodeproj


STEP 2 — Set your Apple ID for signing
---------------------------------------
1. In Xcode, click "CameraAccess" at the top of the left sidebar (the blue project icon)
2. In the middle panel, click the "CameraAccess" target (not the Tests one)
3. Click the "Signing & Capabilities" tab
4. Under "Team", open the dropdown and add your Apple ID if it is not there
   (You may need to click "Add an Account..." and sign in)
5. Xcode will automatically provision the app for your device


STEP 3 — Connect your iPhone
------------------------------
1. Plug your iPhone into the Mac with a USB cable
2. At the top center of Xcode, click the device selector (may say "Any iOS Device" or a simulator name)
3. Select your iPhone from the list
4. If your iPhone asks "Trust This Computer?" — tap Trust


STEP 4 — Hit Run
-----------------
Press the Play button (triangle) in the top left, or press Cmd+R.

The first time it runs, your iPhone may show "Untrusted Developer".
If that happens: iPhone Settings > General > VPN & Device Management > tap your Apple ID > tap Trust.
Then press Run again in Xcode.


What is already done (no action needed)
-----------------------------------------
- Gemini API key is set
- OpoClaw gateway is configured (host, port, token)
- System prompt is set to Thorn's personality
- Bundle ID is set to com.opoclaw.visionclaw
- All app permissions (camera, microphone, local network) are configured


If you are away from home WiFi
--------------------------------
The app is configured to reach your Mac Mini at Opos-Mac-mini.local on your home network.
When away, update the Host field in the app settings to:
  https://keisha-inescapable-clavately.ngrok-free.dev
  Port: 443


Quick test once the app is running
------------------------------------
1. Tap the iPhone mode button on the home screen (to test without glasses)
2. Tap the microphone
3. Say "What time is it?" -- Gemini should respond by voice
4. Say "Send a Telegram message to myself saying VisionClaw is working" -- Thorn should execute it
